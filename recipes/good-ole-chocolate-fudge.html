Good 'Ole Chocolate Fudge

Description

Between you and me, we deserve to treat ourselves once in a while. That's why it would be great idea for you to make this good old fashioned 5-ingredient chocolate fudge recipe--right now. Store these guys in the fridge and feel free to treat yourself whenever you deserve it!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1⁄2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, sliced and softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Grease an 8-inch square baking pan.
  2. In a medium saucepan, place sugar and cocoa powder and pour in milk; Stir until blended.
  3. On medium-high heat, stir constantly until mixture comes to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to low and place a candy thermometer in mixture. Simmer without stirring until reaching 238 degrees F (about 10 minutes)
  5. If you don't have a candy thermometer, drop a small amount of the mixture into cold water. When ready, it forms a soft ball that flattens when removed from water and placed on a flat surface
  6. Remove from heat, cool to 110 degrees without distrurbing the fudge.
  7. Add butter and fvanilla. Beat with wooden spoon until well combined and fudge loses its sheen.
  8. Pour fudge into prepared pan and press to flatten. Cool in room temp. or in fridge
  9. Slice into squares and enjoy!
