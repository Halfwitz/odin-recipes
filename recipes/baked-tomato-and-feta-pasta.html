Baked Tomato and Feta Pasta Dish

Description

One of my favorites to make, this delightful recipe popularized by trending on TikTok is the perfect combination of melted creamy feta and juicy roasted tomatoes. Make an incredible sauce by mixing the two and then mixing in your choice of pasta, fresh herbs, and other extras for the best dish and bake.

Ingredients

  • 2 pints(4 cups) cherry/grape tomatoes
  • 8 ounce block feta chees, drained
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon thyme
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced/grated
  • 1⁄4 cup fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Put the tomatoes and feta in a single layer with a large enough baking dish.
  2. Drizzle on the olive oil, sprinkle on the oregano, thyme, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Preheat oven to 400F and then bake until tomatoes are blistering and feta is golden brown, about 35 minutes.
  4. While baking, cook pasta as directed and drain, keep some of the pasta water
  5. Then, add garlic and basil to the baked dish and mix into a creamy sauce, adding pasta level until desired level of creaminess
  6. Finally, mix in the pasta and enjoy!
