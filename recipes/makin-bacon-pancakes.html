Ingredients

  • Pancake Mix
  • Water
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Read the pancake mix directions.
  2. Follow them...
  3. Sizzle up some bacon to your liking.
  4. Put oil or butter on a heated frying pan. Pour pancake mix to make a circle of your desired size.<./li>
  5. Place several bacon strips onto the uncooked top of the pancake, and flip when the batter is bubbly
  6. Repeat with the rest of the batter and bacon, and enjoy!
